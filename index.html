WBFS Splitter

All files are processed on your computer; none of your files are sent to another computer.

If you are having trouble getting these split wbfs files to display/boot in USB Loader GX/WiiFlow, please read the following guides:

  • r/WiiHacks' Support Megathread
  • USB Loader GX troubleshooting guide (pay attention to the "Reset Cached Titles" part) (some steps also apply to WiiFlow)
  • WiiFlow troubleshooting guide (note: each game doesn't have to be in its own folder).

Do not drag and drop your wbfs file onto the webpage. Just click the button next to "Select wbfs file".
